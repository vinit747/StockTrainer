Welcome to StockWatch, a virtual stock market, where you can trade stocks at the current market price without spending any money. You start off each season with 5000 USD balance. Your goal is to maximize your balance by the end of the season. Each season lasts for a month after which everything is reset. Make sure to sell all your stocks before the season ends, good luck.
